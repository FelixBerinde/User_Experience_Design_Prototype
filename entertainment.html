Merriton Landing

Merriton Landing, located on the north side of Yellow Leaf Bay, is a rapidly developing area that offers a range of entertainment options including art galleries, pubs, and a new dance club.

Coco's Beach

Coco's Beach is a stunning strip of white sand on the west coast of Taniti, a picturesque island in the Pacific. The beach is located in a secluded bay that is surrounded by tall palm trees, giving it a sense of privacy and tranquility. The crystal-clear waters of the bay gently lap at the shore, creating a soothing and relaxing atmosphere that is perfect for sunbathing, swimming, and enjoying water sports.

Helicopter Rides

As the helicopter lifts off the ground, passengers will get a bird's-eye view of Taniti's pristine beaches, crystal-clear waters, and lush tropical forests. From the air, they will be able to see the island's rugged mountain ranges, deep valleys, and dramatic coastline, all while feeling the thrill of flying high above the ground.
