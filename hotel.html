Holiday Inn

Welcome to the Holiday Inn on the beautiful island of Taniti, where you'll experience the ultimate relaxation and tropical getaway. This luxurious resort is nestled on a pristine white sand beach, surrounded by turquoise waters and lush palm trees, offering a true island paradise.

Island Paradise Lodge

Nestled on the idyllic island of Taniti, the "Island Paradise Lodge" is a luxurious retreat for those seeking a peaceful and rejuvenating getaway. Located on the western coast of the island, the lodge boasts breathtaking views of the crystal clear waters of the Pacific Ocean and the verdant hillsides that surround it.

Scotty's Motel

Scotty's Motel is a quaint, family-run lodging located on the picturesque island of Taniti. The motel is situated just a few steps away from the pristine white sand beaches that the island is famous for. The motel is named after its owner, Scott, who inherited the property from his grandfather and has been managing it with his wife and children for over a decade.
